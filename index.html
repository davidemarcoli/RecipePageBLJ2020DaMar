Marble ring cake

What do you need?

100 g Butter
3 Eggs
125 g Sugar
1 pinch Salt
1/2 EL Vanilla sugar
1 1/2 dl Milk
250 g Flour
1 EL Baking Powder

Download our amazing Recipe:

Heat the butter until it melts

Froth the melted butter

Crack the eggs open and place them in another bowl

Add sugar to the eggs

Add a pinch of salt into the bowl

Do the same with baking powder, vanilla sugar, ...

...Milk and...

...Flour

Then put everything together and mix until the mass becomes lighter

Grease the pan and evenly distribute flour in it

Add cocoa powder to half of the mixture and mix it well

Bake in the lower half of the oven for 40 - 45 minutes at 180 degrees
